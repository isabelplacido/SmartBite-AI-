SmartBite AI

Food Awareness & Chronic Disease Prevention with AlexNet

Problem & Motivation Technical Approach Results & Takeaways

The Problem

Chronic diseases like diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease are strongly linked to diet. Many people, especially young adults, eat ultra-processed foods without realizing the long-term risks. Nutrition labels are confusing, and most apps focus on calories instead of food quality.

SmartBite AI aims to help users quickly understand whether a food choice is likely to support or harm their long-term health, using an image of the product as input.

Motivation

  • High rates of obesity and chronic disease are often driven by daily food decisions.
  • People make choices in seconds at the grocery store or on delivery apps.
  • Manual tracking is tedious; an image-based AI assistant could make awareness almost automatic.

This project explores whether a classic convolutional neural network (AlexNet) can be adapted to classify food images into health-related categories, as a small first step toward that kind of assistant.
